Hi 👋 My name is Ben Akram
==========================

Full Stack Developer
--------------------

I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Software Development at Seneca College, with a keen focus on Web Development. My journey in the tech world is driven by my curiosity and the goal to transform challenges into opportunities for growth. 

🎯 Focus: My primary focus is on Web Development. I’m proficient in JavaScript, Python, C/C++, HTML/CSS, and I have experience with frameworks like Next.js, React, and Django. I’m also familiar with Agile/Scrum methodologies. 

🛠️ Tools & Technologies: I have hands-on experience with various developer tools such as AWS, Docker, Git/GitHub, Linux, CI, CD, and Jira. I’ve worked with databases like SQL, MongoDB, S3, and DynamoDB. 

🎓 Education: I’m proud to be a part of Seneca College, where I’ve been recognized on the President’s Honours List. My coursework includes modules like Web Programming for Apps and Services (Next.js), Cloud Computing for Programmers (AWS), Object-Oriented Software Development Using C++, and Advanced Database Technologies. 

🚀 Projects: I’ve worked on several projects, including a full-stack web app for event ticketing management, a scalable AWS-deployed microservice for handling text and image fragments, and a user-friendly web app for browsing the Metropolitan Museum Gallery API. 

💼 Experience: I’ve previously worked as a Medical Device Technician at Inomec Ltd, where I built and upgraded medical devices, programmed Arduino chips, and designed medical devices in SolidWorks. 

🌱 Growth: I believe in continuous learning and expanding my knowledge far beyond the curriculum. I enjoy working in team environments, but I’m also capable of running projects independently. I’m motivated to advance my skills through mentorship and challenging projects. Feel free to explore my repositories and let’s connect!
